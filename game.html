Welcome to Cupcake Fantasy!!!

Directions:Try to get as many cupcakes as you can before time runs out!!

Hello! The goal is to collect as many cupcakes as possible to save your bakery. You were delivering goods around the neighborshood one day when it suddenly started raining cupcakes straight from the sky!! You, as the bakery owner, have decided that this is a good way to make money and save the bakery!! However, your are still required to perfectly catch at least 50 cupcakes withoutt letting them fall to the ground. The cupcakes will all be sold to the spoiled and rich Remo Git , who is celebrating his 18th birthday!! If you manage to catch all 50 and delivers them to the birthday party happening in front of the mansion on time, then enough money can be made to save your bakery! If you are unable to to catch all 50 in a row within 2 minutes, then the game will end and the task is not completed.
